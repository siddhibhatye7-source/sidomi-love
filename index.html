A love letter from Siddhi to Omkar

Open My Heart 💌

My Omkar,

When I look back at 28 March 2019, I smile so softly because that was the day my heart quietly found its person. Our story began in the cutest way, with you asking through a friend instead of proposing directly, and somehow even that became one of the sweetest parts of us. From exchanged numbers to long phone calls, from hidden smiles to silent meetings when no one knew about us, from those school days to now, every chapter has carried your name in my heart.

I still think about our first long drive to Poikhadi hill station, about the feeling of being with you and knowing that something beautiful had started. Since then, we have filled our love with endless conversations, food adventures, silly moments, fights, understanding, and the kind of bond that keeps choosing each other again and again.

7 years are completed, and our 8th year is going on. That sounds like such a big number, but with you, it still feels like the beginning of something that will never stop growing. Through every season, you have been caring, loving, hardworking, and so full of effort. You always try to make me happy. You make space for my feelings. You make me feel protected. You make me feel at home.

With you, I have felt a kind of safety that is rare and beautiful. No one can ever love me the way you do. That is the truth my heart knows without any doubt. Even when we fight, even when life gets messy, even when the days are hard, I still look at you and feel grateful that my love is you.

Thank you for every call, every meeting, every drive, every meal, every apology, every effort, every laugh, and every time you stood beside me. Thank you for being my peace, my comfort, my favorite person, and the one I want in every version of my future.

Whatever happens in our life, we will face it together. We will fight for each other, stand by each other, and grow together. We will hustle through life side by side, chasing our dreams.

One day, we will build a beautiful life together, get married, create our own little world, and have a beautiful family filled with love.

7 years done, 8th going on... and forever still waiting for us.

I love you, always.

Forever yours,
Siddhi
